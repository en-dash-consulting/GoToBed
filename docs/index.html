GoToBed

A gentle, hard-to-ignore — but never trapping — full-screen nudge at the times you choose. Built for bedtime.

Download for macOS

Universal (Apple Silicon & Intel) · macOS 13+ · free & open source

10:30
Time to wind down. Go to bed.
Screens off, lights low.

Multiple schedules

Per-day times, each with its own message, colors, and dismissal.

Soft, not a lock

Esc always dismisses. Cmd-Tab and force-quit never blocked.

Sleep-aware

Skips times missed while your Mac slept — never replays them.

Fully offline

No network, no telemetry, no accounts. Your schedules stay on your Mac.

Installing

  1. Download the latest .dmg above.
  2. Open it and drag GoToBed to Applications.
  3. Launch it — look for the moon icon in your menu bar.

Updating

  1. In the menu-bar menu, choose Check for Updates… (opens the releases page).
  2. Download the newer .dmg and drag GoToBed into Applications, replacing the old copy.
  3. Relaunch.
